# AssetFlow - Enterprise Asset & Resource Management System

## Overview

AssetFlow is an Enterprise Asset & Resource Management System developed for the Odoo Hackathon. The application helps organizations efficiently manage physical assets, shared resources, employee allocations, maintenance workflows, audits, and reports through a centralized ERP platform.

The system replaces manual asset tracking methods such as spreadsheets and paper logs with a structured, secure, and role-based management system.

---

# User Authentication

## Login

Users authenticate using:

- Email
- Password

After successful authentication, the system verifies the user's role and redirects them to the appropriate dashboard.

## Signup

Only Employee accounts can be created through the signup page.

New users are always registered as Employees.

Only the Admin can promote an Employee to:

- Asset Manager
- Department Head

Admin accounts are created manually.
